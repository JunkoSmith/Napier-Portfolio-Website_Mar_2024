    Overview of the Project

    The internship was conducted by an internship team of three students including myself. The Fast Forward project tasked us with redesigning and developing the existing Fast Forward website in a new style to match their newly created gambling education web page current.

    Fast Forward is a volunteer organization based in Edinburgh that provides quality health education, risk-taking behaviour prevention and early intervention programs across Scotland.

    The task of restructuring First Forward's current website with a new design was a great opportunity to apply the knowledge of User-Centred and User-Friendly design, and UX gained from my case study taken so far.
