    Overview of the Project

    This website was created to promote a local photography studio called Riverside Studio, which offers a diverse range of photographic services.

    Originally created through the UCD, UI and SEO approach, the design aimed to increase user traffic and profit for the studio, as well as provide a valuable resource to potential users of their services.

    However, I received feedback that further SEO into the user analysis would make the website more valuable, and I challenged the redesign of the website through SEO and UX design principles again.
