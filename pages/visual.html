    Understanding the everyday quirks of life in Japan

    Designing the Visual Interface Project

    Overview of the Project

    This web-based interactive interface website is called Japan Niche, providing users with specific information and fun informative learning experiences about Japanese culture. Japan Niche targets a wide range of age groups who are interested in learning about Japan and Japanese culture.

    I will therefore introduce the behavioural differences and lifestyles that arise from cultural differences on my website. I thought it would be ideal if users could learn from Japan Niche in advance of their trip, which would increase their desire to visit Japan and help them to refer to it when they visit.

    Design

    Interactive Education

    The purpose of this project was to create an engaging and educational interactive experience that informs users on a specific topic in a fun way, while also creating documentation and high-fidelity prototypes for web-based interactivity.

    The selected content was a website that offers an interactive learning experience about Japan that differentiates it apart from general travel sites, integrating interactive elements with learning methods that were fun for users.
